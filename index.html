Як це працює

You're running a detective agency. This is your most important case yet.

1

The case lands on your desk

Your team receives a physical case file: crime scene photos, interrogation transcripts, lab reports, and a city map. Something happened. Someone did it. The client is paying your agency to find out who — and why.

2

Work the city from your table

The web app is your dispatch center. The city is full of addresses: witnesses who saw something, crime scenes that still hold evidence, the morgue, the police station. Click an address to send an investigator there. Every trip costs from your honorarium — that's taxi fare. Some addresses hold critical evidence. Some are dead ends. You won't know until you go.

3

Build your version

As information comes in, your team builds a theory. Who did it? What was the motive? What was the weapon? The case ends when you're ready — or when the honorarium runs out. Each team submits their answers.

4

The reveal

The host runs a full debrief: here's what actually happened, step by step. Which teams got it right. Who over-investigated. Who nailed it on half the budget.

Every address costs money. Every wrong lead drains your fee.
The winning team isn't the fastest — it's the most precise.
